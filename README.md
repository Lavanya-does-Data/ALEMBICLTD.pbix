# ALEMBICLTD.pbix
This COALINDIA business Power BI project depicts column graph showing sum of volume by turnover in lacks along with two cards showing sum of high & sum of low & a slicer for date; line graph showing sum of volume by turnover in lacks; pie graph showing sum of volume by year.
Data source: Kaggle.
